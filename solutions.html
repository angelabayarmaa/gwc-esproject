Solutions

For Everyone:


  1. 1. Consuming local and seasonal products (this means that you should avoid fruits like watermelon in the winter)


  2. 2. Using bicycles and public transportation, instead of riding a car


  3. 3. Consider traveling by a train instead of car/airplanes if possible


  4. 4. Turning down your heater by just 1 degrees celsius could help

For Businesses:


  1. 1. Committing Net-Zero (This may not be accessible for all businesses, due to the varying costs of offsetting carbon)


  2. 2. Recycle!


  3. 3. Using sustainable supplies, based on certifications such as ISO 14002


  4. 4. Using recycled resources


  5. 5. Switching to Hybrid or fully electric company cars


  6. 6. Investing in Green Energy Equipment
